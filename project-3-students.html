Project Overview & Data Preparation

This project focuses on analyzing student performance data to identify key trends and factors contributing to academic success or failure. The dataset, comprising approximately 1,000 students, was sourced from an Excel file on Kaggle and subjected to a full ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) process.

The columns analyzed included: scores (Math, Reading, Writing), ethnic groups, gender, study assistance data, and parental education levels. By creating a relational database in SQL, I performed data cleaning, structuring, and feature engineering to calculate complex metrics such as the efficiency of study methods and student retention rates.

SQL Query Example

A sample query used for data preparation and calculating average scores grouped by demographics:


/*
Look at IDs 40 through 50 (inclusive). 
What is the desired COUNT of students whose highest score among the three (Math, Reading, Writing)
is actually a MathScore and is not equal to the other scores ?
*/

select count(StudentID) as StudentID
from(
select s.StudentID as StudentID ,s.mathScore as mathScore,s.writingScore as writingScore,
		s.readingScore as readingScore
from student s
where s.mathScore > s.readingScore AND s.mathScore > s.writingScore
)a
where StudentID between 40 and 50

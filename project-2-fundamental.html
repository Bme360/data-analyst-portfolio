Financial Fundamental Analysis Project: Cybersecurity Sector

Comprehensive analysis of three leading cybersecurity companies over 5 years, using SQL modeling and Power BI visualization.

1. Scope and Objective

This project performs a full Fundamental Analysis of three major cybersecurity companies, comparing financial performance, stability, and efficiency across five years. The objective is to identify the strongest and weakest players based on quantitative indicators.

2. Data Sourcing and Preparation

Data was extracted from financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) covering 20 quarters per company. SQL was used to normalize and structure the data, ensuring accurate and comparable results.

3. SQL Data Structuring

Report Type Purpose
Income Statement Profitability and efficiency metrics (Revenue, Gross Margin, Operating Income).
Balance Sheet Financial stability and capital structure (Assets, Liabilities, Equity Ratios).
Cash Flow Statement Liquidity and cash generation capabilities (Free Cash Flow, Operating Cash).

4. Companies in Focus

CrowdStrike Holdings (CRWD)

Zscaler (ZS)

Palo Alto Networks (PANW)

5. Core Metrics Calculated

  • Revenue Growth Rate
  • Gross Margin
  • Operating Margin
  • Net Margin
  • Free Cash Flow Margin
  • Debt-to-Equity Ratio

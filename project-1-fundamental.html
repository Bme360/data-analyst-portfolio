01 Project Overview

This project is the visualization phase of a full Fundamental Analysis. After building the SQL database and calculating all financial metrics, the clean data was imported into Power BI to create highly effective comparative dashboards across a 5-year period (20 quarters per company).

The goal: identify the strongest and weakest cybersecurity companies based on quantitative financial indicators — entirely data-driven, no speculation.

05 Key Performance Indicators

Ratio analysis comparing the three companies side-by-side on profitability and efficiency metrics:

Operating Margin

Measures how much profit a company makes on each dollar of revenue after covering operating costs.

Gross Margin

Revenue minus cost of goods sold — indicates pricing power and production efficiency.

FCF Margin

Free Cash Flow as % of revenue — shows real cash generation ability beyond accounting profit.

Debt-to-Equity

Capital structure health — how much of the company is financed by debt vs. shareholder equity.
